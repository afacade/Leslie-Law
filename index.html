About

Leslie C. Leone

Attorney At Law

(415) 299-0066

Jurisdictions Admitted to Practice: California

Leslie C. Leone, principal attorney at Leone Law Firm, is an experienced California employment attorney and personal injury attorney who helps employees deal with unfair employers and unreasonable insurance companies so they can obtain the justice and compensation they deserve.

Leslie began her legal career as a staff attorney for the Teamsters Union and also worked for the Operating Engineer's Union. As a union attorney she negotiated contracts and grievances for hundreds of union members — giving her an insider's understanding of how institutions make decisions and where leverage actually resides.

She is a San Francisco native who attended UC Berkeley and Golden Gate University Law School. Call Leone Law Firm for a consultation: (415) 299-0066.

The Institutional Advantage

Leslie began her career at the center of the labor movement. As a Staff Attorney and Business Agent for Teamsters Union Local 856, she negotiated improved wages and benefits and represented over 900 members across hospitals, public safety, and private industry.

This "inside-out" understanding of how institutional decisions are made — and where leverage actually resides — is the foundation of her practice.

A Career of Advocacy

As the founder of Leone Law Firm, Leslie has spent over twenty years representing hundreds of clients in employment and personal injury matters. Her deep experience allows her to provide a litigation-aware strategy — helping clients resolve high-stress situations through sophisticated negotiation before they escalate.

The Future of Work

A third-generation San Franciscan, Leslie is focused on the intersection of law and technology. She advises on the strategic shifts caused by AI and emerging workplace dynamics, ensuring her clients are prepared for the future of the employment relationship.

Employees Facing
Workplace Injustice

Leone Law Firm represents employees — not employers. If you're dealing with a workplace dispute, you deserve an attorney who is solely on your side.

My Clients

I represent employees who are facing:

  • Wrongful TerminationYou were fired illegally — in retaliation, in violation of public policy, or in breach of your employment agreement.
  • Workplace DiscriminationYou're being treated unfairly based on race, gender, age, disability, pregnancy, or another protected characteristic.
  • RetaliationYou reported misconduct, took protected leave, or filed a complaint — and your employer punished you for it.
  • Harassment & Hostile Work EnvironmentYou're experiencing sexual harassment or a work environment that has become intolerable.
  • Wage & Hour DisputesYou're not being paid what you're owed — unpaid overtime, missed breaks, or misclassification.
