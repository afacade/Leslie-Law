About

Leslie Leone

With over two decades of experience as an attorney and advisor, Leslie Leone provides the strategic clarity necessary to navigate complex employment disputes and high-stakes transitions.

Contact

San Francisco Bay Area Attorney
Leslie Leone
San Francisco, CA


The Institutional Advantage

Leslie began her career at the center of the labor movement. As a Staff Attorney and Business Agent for Teamsters Union Local 856, she negotiated improved wages and benefits and represented over 900 members across hospitals, public safety, and private industry.

This "inside-out" understanding of how institutional decisions are made — and where leverage actually resides — is the foundation of her consulting practice.

A Career of Advocacy

As the founder of Leslie Leone Law, Leslie has spent nearly twenty years representing hundreds of clients in employment and personal injury matters. Her deep experience with the legal process allows her to provide a "litigation-aware" strategy — helping clients resolve high-stress situations through sophisticated negotiation before they escalate.

She continues to provide select, high-level representation for clients in both employment and personal injury matters.

The Future of Work

A third-generation San Franciscan, Leslie is focused on the intersection of law and technology. She advises on the strategic shifts caused by AI and emerging workplace dynamics, ensuring her clients are prepared for the future of the employment relationship.
